Introduction

We are a team of researchers at the Bern University of Applied Sciences affiliated at the "Generative AI Lab and the Applied Machine Intelligence research group. This website gives an overview of our main research topics and outcomes.

Fairness and Bias in Natural Language Processing

One of our key research topics is the detection and mitigation of bias in natural language processing (NLP). In this context, we are technical partner in the BIAS project. We investigate how stereotypes are reflected in word embeddings and language models, and this impacts downstream applications.

Transformer Models and Augmented Intelligence

We investigate how the recent transformer-based language models can be applied for use cases with societal impact. This contains also the awareness-raising for responsible use of the new technology. We aim at technology that is used as augmented intelligence rather than artificial intelligence, aiming to support humans rather than replacing them.
